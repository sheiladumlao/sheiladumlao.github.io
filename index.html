About

Hello World!

I'm a 3rd Year Management Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Graphic design, product management, and user experience research are areas that I am most interested in.

Around the Internet

Designer & Marketing Coordinator

As the Designer & Creative Coordinator of the 2016 IIE National Student Conference, my main role was to develop the identity and branding of the conference. While starting from scratch and having a ton of creative freedom, I designed the conference logo and curated a colour palette used on all digital and printed assets. I collaborated strongly with the Team Leads and took into account their feedback on all aspects when designing material for the conference.

As a member of the Media Team, I assisted in populating the conference website and managed the social media platforms during the conference.

DealsMe Chatbot

Are you a student in the Waterloo region or a local looking for budget friendly deals in the area? Look no further, the DealsMe chatbot can send you local deals. Just message the DealsMe chatbot a greeting, have a short conversation, and you'll be ready for a night out on a budget.

After many nights of not knowing about daily deals happening in the Waterloo region. Myself and two other co-ops developed this Kik chatbot using Python during Hack Week Winter 2016 to inform us of deals that we were missing out on before.
